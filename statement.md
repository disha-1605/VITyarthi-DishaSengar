## Problem Statement
Students and instructors often spend time creating short quizzes and tracking performance. An automated, easy-to-use tool to generate quizzes, administer them, and show actionable analytics can save time and improve learning outcomes.

## Scope
- Generate quizzes from topic prompts
- Allow students to take quizzes and get instant feedback
- Provide instructors with analytics and exportable reports
- Simple user management and authentication (demo-level)

## Target Users
- Students
- Instructors / Teaching Assistants
- Small educational teams

## High-level Features
- User registration & login (role-based)
- Quiz creation (AI-assisted or template-based)
- Quiz taking and scoring
- Dashboard for analytics and progress tracking
